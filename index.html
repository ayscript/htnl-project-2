Dangers of Drug Abse

Drug abuse, or substance misuse, occurs when someone uses a drug in amounts or ways that are harmful to themselves or others, often to alter their mood or feel better, rather than for a legitimate health reason. This behavior can lead to various health problems and may be associated with criminal or antisocial behavior.

  1. Lung or heart disease
  2. High Chance of getting a stroke
  3. Mental Health Crises
  4. Depression
  5. Liver Failure

Alternatives to drug abuse

Here are some alternative activities you should consider doing:

  1. Regular Exercise
  2. Engaging in coding
  3. Listen or participating in music
  4. Reading good books
  5. Volunteering in the Societies
